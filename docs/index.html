Welcome to the Beauty Box in New Romney

Hi my name is Karen and I run the Beauty Box Hair and Beauty Salon in New Romney, I offer a variety of treatments from a simple haircut, waxing, acrylic nails to hair colouring all from the comfort of my home salon. I have been open for over 9 years in New Romney and have lots of satisfied clients.

I offer all types of treatments and I'm always training to be able to offer more treatments.
